Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Refund & Cancellation Policy governs the terms under which clients may cancel services and request refunds from DimeRepublic. Please read this policy carefully before engaging our services.

1. Cancellation of Services

Clients may cancel their engagement with DimeRepublic under the following conditions:

  • Standard Cancellation: Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days' written notice. Services will continue during the notice period, and applicable fees will remain due.
  • Immediate Cancellation: In cases of material breach by either party, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately with written notice, subject to the cure period specified in the service agreement.
  • Partial Cancellation: Clients may reduce the number of placed professionals with 15 days' written notice. Reduced professionals will complete their final billing cycle.

2. Refund Terms

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Trial Period Refund: New placements include a 14-day trial period. If the placed professional does not meet your requirements during this period, we will provide a replacement at no additional cost or issue a full refund for that placement.
  • Prepaid Services: Prepaid service fees may be refunded on a pro-rata basis for unused services, less any administrative fees.
  • Setup Fees: One-time setup or onboarding fees are non-refundable once services have commenced.
  • Consulting Fees: Fees for consulting or advisory services are non-refundable once the service has been delivered.

3. Notice Periods

The following notice periods apply to various cancellation scenarios:

  • Full Service Cancellation: 30 days written notice required.
  • Partial Team Reduction: 15 days written notice required.
  • Individual Professional Replacement: 5 business days notice during trial period.
  • Breach-Related Termination: 14 days cure period before termination takes effect, unless the breach is incapable of remedy.

4. Service Credits

In certain situations, DimeRepublic may issue service credits instead of refunds:

  • Service credits may be offered for service interruptions exceeding our stated uptime guarantees.
  • Credits are applied to future invoices and are not redeemable for cash.
  • Service credits expire 12 months from the date of issuance.
  • Credits are non-transferable and may only be used by the client to whom they were issued.

5. Exceptions

The following exceptions apply to our refund and cancellation policy:

  • Custom or enterprise agreements may have different cancellation terms as specified in the signed agreement.
  • No refunds will be issued for services already delivered, including completed placements.
  • Clients in material breach of the service agreement are not eligible for refunds.
  • Cancellations requested during the notice period are subject to all accrued fees.

6. Cancellation Process

To cancel services or request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Submit a written cancellation request to hello@dimerepublic.com.
  2. Include your account details, services to be cancelled, and effective date.
  3. Our team will confirm receipt within 2 business days.
  4. We will process the cancellation and any applicable refund within 10 business days.
  5. Refunds will be issued via the original payment method unless otherwise agreed.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with any refund decision, you may escalate the issue to our management team within 30 days of the decision. We will review the matter and respond within 10 business days. If the dispute remains unresolved, it will be handled in accordance with the dispute resolution provisions of our Terms & Conditions.
