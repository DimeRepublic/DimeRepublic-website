Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules and guidelines for using DimeRepublic's services, platforms, and resources. By accessing or using our services, you agree to comply with this policy. Violation of this policy may result in suspension or termination of your access to our services.

1. Prohibited Activities

You may not use our services or platforms to engage in, facilitate, or promote any of the following prohibited activities:

  • Illegal Activities: Any activity that violates applicable local, state, national, or international laws or regulations.
  • Harmful Content: Distributing malware, viruses, ransomware, or any other malicious software or code.
  • Fraud and Deception: Engaging in fraudulent activities, phishing, identity theft, or any form of deception.
  • Harassment and Abuse: Sending threatening, abusive, or harassing communications to any individual or group.
  • Intellectual Property Infringement: Unauthorized use, reproduction, or distribution of copyrighted material, trademarks, or other intellectual property.
  • Unauthorized Access: Attempting to gain unauthorized access to our systems, networks, or data, or those of third parties.
  • Spam and Unsolicited Communications: Sending unsolicited bulk messages, spam, or commercial advertisements.
  • Data Mining: Extracting, scraping, or harvesting data from our platforms without our express written permission.

2. Security Obligations

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your accounts and systems. You agree to:

  • Maintain the confidentiality of your login credentials and not share them with unauthorized individuals.
  • Use strong passwords and enable multi-factor authentication where available.
  • Promptly notify DimeRepublic of any suspected security breach or unauthorized use of your account.
  • Ensure that any devices or systems used to access our services are secure and free from malware.
  • Not attempt to circumvent or disable any security measures implemented by DimeRepublic.

3. Acceptable Use of Talent

Clients engaging talent through DimeRepublic agree to:

  • Provide a safe, respectful, and non-discriminatory work environment for placed professionals.
  • Comply with all applicable labor laws and employment regulations.
  • Not attempt to directly hire placed talent outside of DimeRepublic's engagement terms.
  • Use placed talent only for the roles and responsibilities agreed upon in the service agreement.
  • Not engage placed talent in any activity that would violate this Acceptable Use Policy.

4. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this AUP, you must report it immediately to DimeRepublic. Reports should include sufficient detail to enable investigation, including:

  • Description of the alleged violation.
  • Date and time of the incident.
  • Any evidence or documentation supporting the report.
  • Your contact information for follow-up purposes.

Reports can be submitted to hello@dimerepublic.com.

5. Consequences of Violation

Violation of this AUP may result in any or all of the following actions:

  • Immediate suspension of access to services pending investigation.
  • Termination of your account and service agreement without notice.
  • Removal of any content or materials that violate this policy.
  • Legal action, including referral to law enforcement authorities.
  • Financial liability for any damages or losses caused by the violation.

6. Monitoring and Enforcement

DimeRepublic reserves the right to monitor use of our services for compliance with this AUP. We may investigate any suspected violation and take appropriate action. We are not obligated to monitor all activity but will do so as we deem necessary to protect our services, users, and legal interests.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Acceptable Use Policy, please contact us at hello@dimerepublic.com.
